Announcing the 2021 Call for Code
Awards

Live from New York, on Broadway

Over the last 4 years, the Call for Code Awards has established itself as one of the preeminent award shows celebrating Tech for Good. This year's show promises to be the best yet!

Hosted by Colin Jost of Saturday Night Live, this event will be broadcast live from the New Stages Theatre in Midtown Manhattan - no zoom id required.

Tune in on November 19th as we turn the cameras on the developers and innovators who are at the core the success of Call for Code. Join us as we tell their stories, showcase their talent, and award their contributions to Tech for Good.

This remarkable event will be sure to leave you inspired by what can be accomplished when developers work together to help solve some of the world’s greatest challenges and drive positive change.

Visit our website to learn more and to sign up for e-mail reminders. Review the 42 regional finalists from the Global competition, one of which will be this years Call for Code Challenge winner.
